Will my clinic visit cost me anything?

All services we provide, including specialty referral care at Bellevue Hospital Center, are free of charge.

Can I have my results over the phone?

Results are confidential and cannot be given over the phone. You must book an appointment in order for us to give out the results of your tests. This is to ensure the accurate results are given to the right patient by professional medical staff.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes, we prefer that you have an appointment but same-day appointments are available.

What if I need prescription medications?

Patients who need prescription medication will be assisted in finding free or low-cost options for their medications at the time of their visit.
